About me

My name is Onne Krol. I am currently in my second year of the study Game Developer at Graphic Lyceum Utrecht.
I have been programming since 2016 in that year I first started making basic websites. And in 2020 I started making games because of the study I am attending.
I am very passionate about Gameplay and AI. I want to focus myself more in gameplay in the future because I think I would succeed at that.
I see myself as a very independent person, a flexible person and a good person in communication within a team based environment.

Outside of Programming I’m an Athletic Trainer at Osm 75′ Atletiek where I give training to kids from 4 – 12 years old and learn them the basics of track and field.
I also train for myself about 5 times a week for athletics. I also compete in athletics competitions like the Dutch athletics championship and individual competitions.
This shows that I am good in having a leading role within a team and that I have perseverance because I keep pushing myself for new personal records.
